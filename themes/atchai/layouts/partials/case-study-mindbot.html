mindBot

Life is busy and getting busier. Sometimes technology is the problem causing all kinds of distractions, but it can also be your friend. mindBot is a chatbot that uses introspective questioning and AI to help you organize your mind and concentrate on doing what matters most to you.


Initially developed as a Slack Application, Atchai have been engaged to take the Mindbot concept to every mobile device on the planet, via Twilio's SMS service. Working in short sprints, we have suceeded in getting an MVP to market within 2 weeks, allowing us to start learning about user needs and continually iterate on the product.
