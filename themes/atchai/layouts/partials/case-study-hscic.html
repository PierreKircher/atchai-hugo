The HSCIC (Health and Social Care Information Centre) is the public body that collects, processes and publishes data covering all levels of the UK’s National Health Service for the Department of Health. The NHS is the world’s 5th largest employer, so it generates a lot of data!

Atchai won the project with a competitive bid proposing an open source technology stack including Drupal, integrated tightly with our own Dataseed platform. 200GB of data is imported quarterly into Dataseed’s RESTful API, enabling the public and clinicians to visually interrogate healthcare outcomes, admissions, prescriptions, and administrative datasets through a single, simple interface.
